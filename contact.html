Becoming a Web Developer

This is my first post on the first blog that I have ever created. First I'd like to give a big shout-out to CodePen for the amazing website that allows you you access everything from a single dashboard. I am learning this because the company I work for could use someone else helping with the development of the app that we are creating along with website development.

I am so eager to learn everything that I can, but I need to learn to slow down and actually play with the language. That's all it is, you're learning how to tell your computer what to do, in ENGLISH! No fancy accent signs, no rolling of the tongue on those 'r"s', none of it! I wish that I could spend all day every day learning and playing with the code, but, for now, it's time to move on,
